paper-reproductions

PythonPyTorchDockerApptainer

paper-reproductions is a monorepo of from-scratch PyTorch reproductions of selected machine-learning papers. Each folder holds one paper's model, loss, and dataset plus a short write-up of the method and results; the heavy lifting - training loop, validation, caching, component wiring, and hook orchestration - is delegated to the torchestrator runner and the registry-pattern factory, so a reproduction is just domain code and a declarative config.

It currently spans differentiable combinatorial optimization and structured prediction (SoftSort, differentiable sorting/ranking, Fenchel-Young losses, SparseMAP, blackbox-solver differentiation, low-rank SDP), gradient-approximation and implicit-differentiation methods, optimal transport (Sinkhorn / Wasserstein), and self-supervised learning, with reproducible Docker and Apptainer environments. It consolidates the earlier ot-reproduce and ssl-reproduce projects.
