paper-reproductions

PythonPyTorchDockerApptainer

paper-reproductions is a monorepo of from-scratch PyTorch reproductions of selected machine-learning papers. Each folder holds one paper's model, loss and dataset plus a short write-up of the method and results; the heavy lifting (training loop, validation, caching, component wiring, hook orchestration) is delegated to the torchestrator runner and the registry-pattern factory, so a reproduction is just domain code and a declarative config. Each paper FROMs a shared env.torchfile and runs with a single command; reproducible Docker and Apptainer environments are provided. It consolidates the earlier ot-reproduce and ssl-reproduce projects.
