Frequency-Weighted Neural Kalman Filters

Dogan, A. H., Demirel, B. U., Holz, C. · International Conference on Robotics and Automation (IEEE ICRA) · 2026

Classical Kalman filters assume white Gaussian sensor noise and degrade when that noise is concentrated in particular frequency bands. FW-NKF addresses this by inserting a learnable IIR filter on the Kalman innovations and training it with spectral supervision, so the filter learns frequency-selective denoising guided by the observation model.

Across synthetic systems (the Lorenz attractor and a nonlinear pendulum) and real-world tracking — EuRoC MAV IMU odometry and UWB–IMU human pose estimation — FW-NKF consistently outperforms classical and neural Kalman baselines under frequency-localized noise.
