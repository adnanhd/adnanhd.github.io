Beyond subjectivity: Continuous cybersickness detection using eeg-based multitaper spectrum estimation

Demirel, B. U., Dogan, A. H., Rossie, J., Möbus M., Holz, C. · IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics · 2025

Cybersickness in virtual reality is usually assessed with subjective questionnaires. This work instead derives a continuous, objective measure from brain activity: EEG signals are processed with multitaper spectrum estimation to produce robust spectral features, which a deep network maps to a cybersickness estimate over time.

The paper releases a public EEG-in-VR dataset and shows that the multitaper-based pipeline tracks cybersickness more reliably than subjective baselines. Published at IEEE TVCG 2025, in collaboration with ETH Zürich's Sensing, Interaction & Perception Lab.
